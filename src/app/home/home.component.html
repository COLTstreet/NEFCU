Welcome!

Let us introduce ourselves, we are Natrium Employees Federal Credit Union. Credit Unions are not for profit financial cooperatives that exist to serve their members. When you join a credit union you become a member and an owner. Credit Unions return their profits back to their members in the form of lower interest rates and higher dividends on deposits.

We offer a variety of services from checking and saving accounts to competitive rates on Certificates of Deposit. We have Vacation and Christmas Club Accounts that you can have automatically deposited so you never have to worry about those expenses ! We also offer VISA credit cards with a VERY competitive rate that cannot be beat!

There are a multitude of loan options available to you also from farm equipment, boats, ATV's or a new car with competitive interest rates and the ease of applying for those loans at NEFCU with the option of payroll deductions that can be utilized you simply cannot go wrong. With a wide spectrum of options we can help you succeed in your financial goals!!
