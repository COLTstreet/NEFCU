Other Services

Bill Payer

Monthly bill paying at a charge of $20.00 per month. Bill must be presented to the Credit Union at least 10 days prior to the due date. Checks will be issued against your account and mailed to the proper creditor.

Skip A Pay

Skip a loan payment one time per year, per loan. A $20.00 fee will be assessed on each loan skipped. One month notice prior required when opting to participate in this program. No limit on the number of loans to be skipped.

Voice Response System

We have an audio teller that is available 24 hours a day for your added convenience.

  • Call 304-455-4881 and enter your pass code information.
  • If you do not have a pass code, contact the Credit Union to have one set up.
