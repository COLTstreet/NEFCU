Visa Credit Cards

Our debit cards look like a VISA credit card but works just like writing a "draft" from your share draft account. Use your card at any merchant that accepts VISA and your transaction will be automatically debited from your share draft account.

We offer Visa EMV Debit Cards for your added security. The new EMV Chip cards make it virtually impossible to counterfeit and make travel much easier with acceptance at over 130 countries worldwide. EMV chip cards do everything a magnetic reader card will do but with added security, plus you are covered by VISA'S Zero Liability Policy*.

*VISA'S ZERO LIABILITY POLICY DOES NOT APPLY TO CERTAIN COMMERCIAL CARD AND ANONYMOUS PREPAID CARD TRANSACTIONS OR TRANSACTIONS NOT PROCESSED BY VISA. CARDHOLDERS MUST USE CARE IN PROTECTING THEIR CARD AND NOTIFY THEIR ISSUING FINANCIAL INSTITUTION IMMEDIATELY OF ANY UNATHORIZED USE.
