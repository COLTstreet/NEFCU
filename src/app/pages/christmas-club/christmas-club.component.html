Christmas Club Account

Christmas Club accounts are a great way to prepare for that inevitable Holiday crush. Take the stress out of the Holiday season and the fear of what the mailman may bring in January by making small contributions all year long to your Christmas Club.

Christmas Clubs begin on October 15th.

  • Funds are deposited directly into your saving account unless we have been instructed otherwise.
  • Christmas Clubs earn dividends quarterly.
  • Withdrawals may be made against this account for a $10.00 service fee.
  • You may open a Christmas Club at any time.
  • This can be set up as a payroll deduction.
