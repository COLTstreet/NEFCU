Payroll Deduction

Payroll deduction is a great tool in allocating funds from your paycheck directly to your accounts or to a loan.

Payroll deduction allows you to:

  • Have peace of mind that should you be unable to get to the Credit Union your payments are still on time.
  • Let's you "pay yourself first" by putting money directly into your savings.

*Payroll deduction and Direct Deposit are two different entities. In Direct Deposit your entire payroll check would be deposited as directed.
