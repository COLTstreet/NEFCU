Share (Savings) Account

We offer our members share savings accounts. Deposits into these accounts are regarded as "shares" since the holders of the account are owners or a member of the credit union.

Share accounts are opened to establish your membership into the credit union and must maintain a $5.00 balance. Once your account is opened it allows you the opportunity to take advantage of all the other services this credit union offers.

This also makes you eligible to volunteer for a seat on our board of directors and to vote in our annual elections held each year at our annual meeting to appoint leadership.

As a member, this automatically makes your immediate family (i.e., mother, father, brother, sister, spouse, and children) eligible for membership and gives them the ability to take advantage of our services.

"SHARE" SAVINGS is a great way to start building you savings and preparing for your future.

Your share savings account is your “share” in our credit union, plus a great tool to jump start your savings!

  • $5.00 minimum balance
  • Dividends posted quarterly
  • No monthly maintenance fee
  • Individual deposit accounts covered by NCUA up to $250,000
