Share Secured Loans

A shared secured loan is one that uses the assets in a share account, otherwise known as a savings account, to back up the loan. When you take out a shared secured loan, the equivalent assets within your savings account are frozen and become available again as you pay off the loan.

There are some great benefits to a Share Secured Loan:

  • They are a great way to build credit and typically relatively easy to qualify for since you use your funds to secure the loan the risk to your financial institution is minimal.
  • Give you extra incentive to repay the loan: as you repay the loan it will free up those monies in your savings allowing you to rebuild your savings account.
  • Share Secured Loans can be used for any purpose.
  • Share Secured Loans will typically give you the best interest rate, with a lower interest rate you save substantially in interest payments.
