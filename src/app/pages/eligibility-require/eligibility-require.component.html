Eligibility Requirements

It's easy to join Bayer Heritage Federal Credit Union if you are eligible! Just open a Share Account with a $5.00 deposit and you are a member with all the benefits and privileges.

Membership Eligibility Requirements

  • Employees of Bayer Corporation that are specifically named in the NHFCU Charter
  • Immediate family of existing members.
  • Individuals who live, work, worship or attend school in, or businesses and other legal entities located in
    • Wetzel County, WV
    • Tyler County, WV
    • Monroe County, WV
  • Individuals who live, work, worship or attend school in, or businesses and other legal entities located in specific areas* of the following counties
    • Marshall County, WV
    • Blemont County, OH
    • Licking County, OH
    • Berkeley County, SC
    • Charleston County, SC
    • Muskingum County, OH

*Specific Areas: Please contact your local branch of the Credit Union for specific areas in the listed counties.
