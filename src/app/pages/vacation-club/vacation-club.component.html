Vacation Club Account

Everyone needs some R & R ! Whether it's a quick weekend getaway to grab a break from daily stress, a family trip to the beach or a romantic trip for two there's no better way to plan for those trips than with a vacation club.

  • You can open a vacation club at any time
  • Vacation Club accounts pay quarterly dividends.
  • You may withdrawal funds up to 4 times per calendar year. Penalty for additional withdrawals is $10.00.
  • This can be set up as a payroll deduction.
