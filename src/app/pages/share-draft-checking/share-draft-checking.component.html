Share Draft (Checking)

Your Share Draft Account in a Credit Union refers to the equivalent of a traditional checking account in a bank. A Share Draft allows you to write a "draft" from your account either traditionally with a check or with a debit card. Two types of agreements are offered:

Share Draft Agreement

If a member has the Share Draft Agreement, there will be a $25 charge per draft that is returned for NSF (Non-Sufficient Funds). If the member is notified and funds are transferred from another account, the fee is still applied.

Share Draft Agreement with Overdraft Transfer Clause

If the Overdraft Transfer Clause is selected, and the member has the funds available in a Natrium Employees Federal Credit Union savings account, funds will be automatically transferred from savings to share drafts. A fee of $5 per day of needed transfer will apply.

Important features in Share Draft Checking:

  • No minimum balance
  • No service charge
  • We offer Debit Cards at no additional fee
  • Overdraft Protection Option
  • Convenient check reorder
